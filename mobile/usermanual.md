# LocaPin User Manual

Welcome to **LocaPin**, your interactive guide to exploring the rich heritage and vibrant culture of San Juan City! This manual provides step-by-step instructions for all features available to both Tourists and Administrators.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 App Launch & Authentication
- **Initial Setup**: Upon first launch, the app will perform a session check. If you're not logged in, you'll be directed to the authentication screen.
- **Login**: Enter your email and password. Use the "Remember me" option to stay signed in.
- **Sign-Up**: Switch to the "Sign-Up" tab to create a new account.
  - **Mandatory Agreement**: You must check the "I agree to the EULA and Terms" box. You can read the full documents by clicking the linked text.
  - **Social Login**: Quick access buttons for Google and Facebook are available (currently in placeholder/Coming Soon state).

### 1.2 Dashboard Navigation
The **Tourist Dashboard** is your hub for discovery. From here, you can access:
- **Map**: The core exploration experience.
- **Attractions**: A quick-browse list of destinations.
- **Favorites**: Your personal collection of saved places.
- **Profile**: Manage your personal information.

---

## 2. Exploring San Juan City

### 2.1 Using the Interactive Map
- **Map Sectors**: The map is divided into clickable sectors. Each sector represents a specific neighborhood or area of interest in San Juan.
- **Tapping a Sector**: Tap any colored area on the map to view a summary of attractions in that sector via the bottom sheet.
- **Interacting with the Map**: You can pinch to zoom or drag to move around the high-resolution city map.

### 2.2 Discovering Attractions
- **Bottom Sheet Summary**: When a sector is selected, a sheet appears showing the number of attractions available.
- **View All**: Click "View Attractions" in the bottom sheet to open a grid of all places in that sector.
- **Attraction Details**: Tap any attraction card to see:
  - High-quality imagery.
  - Distance from STI Sta. Mesa (reference point).
  - A detailed description of the location's history and significance.

---

## 3. Premium Features

### 3.1 Removing Advertisements
- **Ad Experience**: Free users may see banner ads at the bottom of the screen or occasional interstitial ads.
- **Go Ad-Free**: Click the "Block" icon (circle with a slash) in the Map screen's top bar to open the "No Ads" prompt.
- **Payment Flow**: Confirm the one-time ₱99.00 payment through the mock Google Play interface to permanently remove all advertisements.

---

## 4. Administrative Features (Admin Role Only)

If you are logged in as an Administrator, you have access to the **Admin Dashboard**:
- **Map Sector Management**: Define the boundaries and names of city sectors.
- **Attraction Content**: Upload new attractions, set their coordinates, and manage descriptions/images.
- **System Sync**: Changes made by admins are reflected instantly for all LocaPin users.

---

## 5. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I log out?**
**A**: Open the side menu (hamburger icon) or tap the logout icon in the Map screen top bar.

**Q: I forgot my password, what should I do?**
**A**: On the Login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" to start the recovery process.

**Q: Why is the "Create Account" button disabled?**
**A**: Ensure you have checked the EULA and Terms agreement box and that all fields (Name, Email, Password) are correctly filled.
